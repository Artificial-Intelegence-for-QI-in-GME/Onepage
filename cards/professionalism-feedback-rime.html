Professionalism Feedback (RIME)

Provides RIME-based professionalism feedback for a 3rd-year med student.

Create feedback for a 3rd-year medical student who demonstrates empathy and professionalism but needs improvement in physical exam technique. Use the RIME framework. --- **Use Case:** Feedback **Learner Level:** MS3 **Output Format:** Short paragraph, <150 words **Constraints:** Use RIME terms like Reporter/Interpreter, Avoid vague praise **Tags:** RIME, feedback, MS3, professionalism **Rubric:** RIME **Variation Request:** Offer two tone styles: formal and coaching.
