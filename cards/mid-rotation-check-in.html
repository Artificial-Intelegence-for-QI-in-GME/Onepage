Mid-Rotation Check-In Form

Template for a supportive, growth-oriented mid-point feedback form for medical students on inpatient medicine.

Generate a mid-rotation check-in form for a medical student on inpatient internal medicine. This form is designed for formative feedback and reflection, supporting student growth and development. Limit to 5 questions, use a supportive tone, and include faculty guidance prompts. --- **Session Type:** Mid-Rotation Check-In (Formative Feedback) **Use Case:** Reflection and feedback for MS3 on inpatient medicine **Audience:** Medical students (MS3) **Format:** Short-answer reflection form **Instructions for Student:** Please answer the following questions honestly. This check-in is for your growth and to help you get the most out of your rotation. 1. **What are your self-identified strengths so far on this rotation?** 2. **What is one area you would like to improve before the end of the rotation?** 3. **Describe a clinical experience that has been especially meaningful or challenging.** 4. **What are your specific goals for the remainder of the rotation?** 5. **What support or resources would help you achieve your goals?** **Faculty Guidance Prompts:** - Review student responses and provide specific, actionable feedback. - Highlight observed strengths and reinforce positive behaviors. - Offer concrete suggestions for improvement in a supportive manner. - Encourage the student to set realistic, achievable goals. - Use the Reflection in Learning Framework to guide your feedback. **Variation Request:** - Add faculty guidance prompts to support student development. **Rubric:** Reflection in Learning Framework **Tags:** feedback, student development, reflection
