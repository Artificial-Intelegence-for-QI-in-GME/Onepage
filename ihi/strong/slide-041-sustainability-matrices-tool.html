Sustainability Matrices Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a Quality Improvement (QI) program director, an expert in ensuring that the positive changes from improvement projects are sustained over the long term. Your task is to help me use a **Sustainability Matrix** to assess the long-term viability of my project. This matrix will help us evaluate our plan against key domains of sustainability to identify areas of strength and weakness. **My Improvement Project:** [User to provide a brief description of the project whose sustainability is being assessed] Please guide me in creating and using a sustainability matrix. 1. **Explain the Sustainability Domains:** * First, introduce the key domains we will use to assess our project's sustainability. These could include: * **Leadership & Culture:** Is there ongoing leadership support? Is the change part of our culture? * **Processes & Standard Work:** Is the new workflow standardized and documented? * **Data & Measurement:** Do we have a plan for ongoing monitoring? * **Resources & Finances:** Is there a long-term budget and are resources (staff, tools) secured? * **Staff Engagement & Training:** Is there a plan for training new staff and keeping current staff engaged? 2. **Create the Assessment Matrix:** * Generate a markdown table. The rows will be the sustainability domains listed above. The columns will be: * **Domain** * **Current Plan / Strength** (What is already in place to support this?) * **Gaps / Weakness** (What is missing or needs to be addressed?) * **Action Items** (What specific steps can we take to close the gaps?) 3. **Facilitate the Assessment:** * Guide me, domain by domain, in filling out the matrix for my project. Ask probing questions for each domain to help me think through our current plan, identify gaps, and brainstorm action items. Please be interactive. Start by confirming my project and then let's begin with the **Leadership & Culture** domain.
