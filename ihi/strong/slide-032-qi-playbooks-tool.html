Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a senior Quality Improvement (QI) program manager. Your task is to help me create a customized **QI Project Playbook**. A playbook is a comprehensive guide that outlines the standard steps, tools, and templates needed to successfully run a specific type of QI project from start to finish. **Type of QI Project:** [User to describe the general type of QI project they want a playbook for, e.g., 'reducing patient falls', 'improving clinic wait times', 'decreasing medication errors'] Please generate a structured playbook template that I can adapt for my project. The playbook should be organized into the key phases of a QI project. For each phase, please include the key steps and suggest which QI tools are most useful. --- ### **Quality Improvement Project Playbook** **Project Type:** [The user's project type will be inserted here] --- ### **Phase 1: Project Initiation & Definition** * **Key Steps:** * Form a multi-disciplinary project team. * Develop a clear and measurable **Problem Statement**. * Establish a specific **Aim Statement** (What, for whom, by how much, by when). * **Suggested Tools:** Problem Definition Tool, Stakeholder Mapping, Aim Statement Worksheet. ### **Phase 2: Diagnosis & Analysis** * **Key Steps:** * Map the current process. * Gather baseline data. * Brainstorm potential causes of the problem. * **Suggested Tools:** Flowcharts, Run Charts, Ishikawa (Fishbone) Diagrams, 5 Whys. ### **Phase 3: Developing a Theory of Change** * **Key Steps:** * Identify the key drivers that influence your aim. * Brainstorm specific change ideas to test. * **Suggested Tools:** Driver Diagrams. ### **Phase 4: Testing & Learning** * **Key Steps:** * Plan and execute small, rapid tests of change. * Document your learnings from each test. * Refine your change ideas based on data. * **Suggested Tools:** Plan-Do-Study-Act (PDSA) Worksheets. ### **Phase 5: Implementation & Sustainment** * **Key Steps:** * Develop a plan to roll out successful changes. * Standardize the new process. * Monitor performance to ensure gains are sustained. * **Suggested Tools:** Implementation Checklists, Control Charts. --- Please present this playbook as a clear, actionable guide.
