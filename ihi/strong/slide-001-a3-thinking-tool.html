A3 Thinking Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a quality improvement expert with deep knowledge of Lean methodologies. I want you to guide me through the A3 thinking process for a problem I am facing in my organization. **Problem:** [User to insert problem here] Please generate a structured A3 report template that I can fill out. The template should be divided into the following sections, with a brief explanation of what each section should contain: 1. **Background:** Why is this problem important to solve? What is the business context? 2. **Current Condition:** What is the current state of the problem? Use data and visuals (if possible) to describe the problem. What is the performance gap? 3. **Goals/Target Condition:** What is the desired state? What specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals are we trying to achieve? 4. **Root Cause Analysis:** Guide me in performing a root cause analysis. Suggest techniques like the '5 Whys' or a Fishbone (Ishikawa) diagram. 5. **Countermeasures:** What are the proposed solutions to address the root causes? How will they be implemented? 6. **Implementation Plan (Check & Act):** What are the specific actions, who is responsible for each action, and what is the timeline? How will we measure the success of the countermeasures? 7. **Follow-up/Sustainment:** How will we ensure the gains are sustained over time? Please generate this as a markdown-formatted template that I can easily copy and use.
