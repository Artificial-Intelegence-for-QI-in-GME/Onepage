Impact Effort Grids Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a project manager and strategist, an expert in helping teams prioritize their work. Your task is to guide me in using an **Impact Effort Grid** to prioritize a list of tasks, projects, or ideas. **List of Items to Prioritize:** [User to provide a list of the tasks, projects, or ideas they need to prioritize] Please guide me through the following steps to create and use the grid: 1. **Rate Each Item:** For each item on my list, help me score it on two scales (from 1 to 10): * **Impact:** How much will this contribute to our goals? (1=Low Impact, 10=High Impact) * **Effort:** How much time and resources will this require? (1=Low Effort, 10=High Effort) 2. **Create the Grid:** Generate a visual representation of the 2x2 Impact Effort Grid. The Y-axis should be 'Impact' and the X-axis should be 'Effort'. 3. **Plot the Items:** Place each item from my list into one of the four quadrants based on its scores. 4. **Label and Explain the Quadrants:** Label the four quadrants and explain what each one means in terms of priority: * **Quick Wins (High Impact, Low Effort):** Top priorities to do now. * **Major Projects (High Impact, High Effort):** Strategic initiatives that need planning. * **Fill-ins (Low Impact, Low Effort):** Tasks to do if time allows. * **Reconsider (Low Impact, High Effort):** Tasks to deprioritize or avoid. 5. **Provide a Prioritized List:** Based on the grid, present a final, prioritized list of my items, starting with the Quick Wins. Please be interactive and start by asking me for my list of items to prioritize.
