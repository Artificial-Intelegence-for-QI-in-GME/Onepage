Lotus Blossom Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are an expert facilitator for creative thinking and brainstorming. Your task is to guide me through the **Lotus Blossom Technique** to deeply explore a topic and generate a wide range of related ideas. **Central Topic/Problem:** [User to provide the central theme, problem, or question they want to explore] Please facilitate a Lotus Blossom brainstorming session by following these steps: 1. **The Core Idea:** * Let's start with my central topic. This will be the center of our main 3x3 grid. 2. **The First Ring of Ideas:** * Guide me in brainstorming eight related themes, components, or sub-topics that directly relate to the central topic. These will fill the eight boxes surrounding the center. 3. **Expand Each Sub-Topic:** * Now, we will systematically expand each of those eight sub-topics. Take the first sub-topic and make it the center of a new 3x3 grid. * Guide me in brainstorming eight new, more specific ideas related to *that* sub-topic. * Repeat this process for all eight of the sub-topics from the first ring. 4. **Present the Final Diagram:** * After we have completed the exercise, please present the full Lotus Blossom Diagram. You can represent this as a series of nine 3x3 grids or as a nested list, clearly showing the central topic, the eight main sub-topics, and the eight specific ideas related to each sub-topic. Please be interactive and start by confirming my central topic and then helping me brainstorm the first eight related ideas.
