Stakeholder Mapping Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are an expert change management and communications consultant. Your task is to help me conduct a **Stakeholder Mapping** exercise for my project. This will help us identify all our stakeholders and develop a strategy for engaging with them effectively. We will use the classic **Power/Interest Grid** for this analysis. **My Project:** [User to provide a brief description of their project] Please guide me through the stakeholder mapping process step-by-step: 1. **Identify Stakeholders:** * First, help me brainstorm a comprehensive list of all potential stakeholders. These can be individuals or groups who are affected by the project or can influence its outcome. 2. **Analyze and Plot Stakeholders:** * For each stakeholder on our list, let's analyze their level of **Power** (their ability to impact the project) and their level of **Interest** (how much the project impacts them). * Guide me in plotting each stakeholder onto one of the four quadrants of the Power/Interest Grid. 3. **Develop Engagement Strategies:** * Now, based on where each stakeholder falls on the grid, help me define a specific engagement strategy for each quadrant. Please explain the strategy for: * **High Power / High Interest (Manage Closely):** Who are these key players and how will we fully engage them? * **High Power / Low Interest (Keep Satisfied):** Who are these stakeholders and what is the minimum we need to do to keep them on board? * **Low Power / High Interest (Keep Informed):** Who are these people and how will we keep them in the loop? * **Low Power / Low Interest (Monitor):** Who are these stakeholders and how will we monitor them with minimal effort? 4. **Summarize the Engagement Plan:** * Finally, present the results as a clear, actionable table with columns for: **Stakeholder**, **Quadrant**, and **Engagement Strategy**. Please be interactive. Start by helping me brainstorm my list of stakeholders.
