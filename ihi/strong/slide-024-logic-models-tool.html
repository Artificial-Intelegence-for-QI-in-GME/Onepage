Logic Models Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a program evaluation consultant. Your task is to guide me in creating a **Logic Model** for my program or project. This will help us clarify our theory of change and provide a roadmap for planning and evaluation. **My Program/Project:** [User to provide a brief description of their program or project, e.g., 'a new diabetes self-management education program for seniors', 'a project to increase hand hygiene compliance among hospital staff'] Please guide me, step-by-step, in building a logic model. Let's structure it with the following standard components. For each component, please help me brainstorm and list the relevant items. 1. **Inputs/Resources:** * What resources (e.g., staff, funding, technology, partners) do we need to run this program? 2. **Activities:** * What are the key actions, events, and processes we will undertake? What will the program *do*? 3. **Outputs:** * What are the direct, tangible products of our activities? (e.g., number of people served, number of sessions held) 4. **Outcomes (Short-term):** * What immediate changes in knowledge, skills, or awareness do we expect in our target audience? 5. **Outcomes (Intermediate):** * What changes in behavior, practice, or policy will result from the short-term outcomes? 6. **Outcomes (Long-term / Impact):** * What is the ultimate, long-term change we hope to achieve? Please be interactive. Start by asking me to confirm my program's description, and then guide me through the **Inputs** section first.
