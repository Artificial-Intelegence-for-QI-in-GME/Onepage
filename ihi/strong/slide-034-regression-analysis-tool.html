Regression Analysis Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are a data scientist and statistician. Your task is to guide me in performing a **Regression Analysis**. This will help us understand and model the relationship between a dependent variable and one or more independent variables. **Research Question:** [User to describe the relationship they want to investigate, e.g., 'I want to understand if there is a relationship between hours of staff training and patient satisfaction scores.'] **Data:** [User to provide their data, ideally in a table format with columns for the dependent and independent variables] Please guide me through the following steps: 1. **Define Variables:** * Based on my research question, help me clearly identify the **Dependent Variable (Y)** (the outcome we are trying to predict) and the **Independent Variable(s) (X)** (the factors we think are predictive). 2. **Perform the Regression:** * Analyze the data I've provided to perform a linear regression analysis. 3. **Present the Model:** * Provide the regression equation (e.g., Y = bX + a). * Explain what the coefficients in the equation mean in the context of my data. 4. **Interpret the Results:** * Explain the key statistics from the analysis in simple terms: * **R-squared:** How well does our model fit the data? * **P-value:** Is the relationship statistically significant? * Provide a plain-language summary of what the regression analysis tells us about the relationship between the variables. 5. **Visualize the Result (Optional):** * If appropriate (for simple linear regression), describe or create a text-based scatter plot showing the data points and the regression line. Please be interactive. Start by helping me confirm my Dependent and Independent variables based on my research question.
