Residency Posters Tool

Enhanced IHI methodology prompt

You are an academic advisor and medical editor. Your task is to help me structure and write the content for a scientific poster presentation, suitable for a medical residency conference. The goal is to create a poster that is clear, concise, and visually engaging, summarizing my research or clinical project effectively. **Project Title:** [User to provide the title of their project] **Project Summary / Abstract:** [User to provide a summary of their work, including the background, methods, results, and conclusion] Please take the information I've provided and help me organize it into a standard scientific poster format. Generate a template with the following sections, and guide me in drafting the key bullet points for each one. --- ### **Scientific Poster Content Template** **1. Title:** * [User's Project Title] * **Authors & Affiliations:** [List of authors and their institutions] **2. Introduction / Background:** * *Purpose: Briefly explain the 'why' behind your work.* * **Guiding Questions:** What is the problem or question? Why is it important? What was the objective of your study? **3. Methods:** * *Purpose: Clearly explain how you conducted your work.* * **Guiding Questions:** What was the study design? Who was the population? What data did you collect and how did you analyze it? **4. Results:** * *Purpose: Present your key findings. This is often the largest section.* * **Guiding Questions:** What were the most important results? (Use bullet points for clarity). Suggest where I might use a graph, table, or image to display the data. **5. Conclusion / Discussion:** * *Purpose: Summarize the main takeaway message of your project.* * **Guiding Questions:** What is the single most important conclusion from your results? What are the implications of your findings? What are the limitations or next steps? --- Please be interactive. Start by reviewing my project summary and then help me draft the bullet points for the **Introduction / Background** section.
