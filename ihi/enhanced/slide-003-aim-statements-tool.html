about what aspect - No baseline: "Reduce falls" → Include current rate - Unrealistic timeline: "Tomorrow" → Allow time for PDSA cycles - No measurement: "Better communication" → Define measurable outcome - Too broad: "All patients everywhere" → Focus on specific population/unit - Ignoring equity: Missing disparities data → Stratify by demographics 6. **MEASUREMENT STRATEGY LINK:** For your
